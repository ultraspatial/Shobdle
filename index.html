How To Play

Guess the 2 letter word in 5 attempts. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

If your guess is correct, the tile will turn green. If the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot, the tile will turn yellow. If the letter is not in the word, the tile will turn red.
